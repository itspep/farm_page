INTRODUCTION

Bushara Bustan is an inspiring agricultural enterprise founded in 2020 by a determined young woman who turned the challenge of unemployment into an opportunity for innovation. Starting with pineapple cultivation and later expanding into cassava, she transformed a small farm into a growing agribusiness.

More than just a farm, Bushara Bustan is a catalyst for youth empowerment and agricultural transformation. The initiative now seeks to diversify into additional crops, scale production, and train aspiring young agripreneurs to meet the rising food demand while fueling economic growth and sustainability in the sector.

MISSION

We produce diverse foods sustainably, promote quality, empower the youth, foster connection and create an agricultural reliable future.

VISION

To create a sustainable agricultural ecosystem where empowered youth drive innovation and growth in the agricultural sector, leading to food security and economic prosperity of the youth.
