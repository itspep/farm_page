Meet Our Founder

Young. Passionate. Rooted in Agriculture!

Bushira Farm Produce was founded by a determined and visionary young woman who set out to make a lasting impact in one of Ghana’s most essential sectors — agriculture. With a deep belief that food is a fundamental human need, she was inspired to be part of the movement that feeds the nation and sustains life.

Her journey began in 2019, funded entirely through her National Service savings. With just one crop — pineapple — she began her first cultivation. That spark quickly grew into something more as she expanded to include cassava, ginger, and pepper. Today, she continues to expand her crop range and scale her operations.

Born and raised in the Upper East Region of Ghana, she attended Tuffour Red Sox International School and completed in 2010. She later enrolled at Tamale Senior High School, graduating in 2013. She went on to earn a diploma in French at the Ghana Institute of Languages in 2015, followed by a Higher National Diploma in Bilingual Secretaryship, and later, a BSc and MPhil in Environmental Sciences.
